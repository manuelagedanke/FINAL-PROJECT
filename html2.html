Relationships between students

Good relationships between teenagers at school are essential for their social, emotional, academic, and mental well-being. These relationships shape how they interact with the world around them, help them develop important life skills, and provide the foundation for positive future interactions.

Fostering an environment where teens can build strong, supportive relationships is a key factor in promoting their overall development and happiness during these formative years.

It is very important for schools to incite good relationships between students for them to feel comfortable, happy and as a result succeed academically and personally
