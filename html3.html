The importance of this page

The institution “Ivy Thomas Memorial School” is having a problem. Students are suffering bad relationships between them and there are problems between teachers and students.

“Be the hand that lifts others up”

Bad consequences are clearly affecting the school in lots of different ways:

  • There is a bad environment because students are stressed and unhappy
  • There is a decrease in academic performance due to demotivation and little desire of attending school
  • Students’ self-esteem is low and they have little confidence because they have very little support from their
    • classmates.
  • Problems between students and teachers generate tension and anger.

    		•

    It is important to conscientize students and foment respect towards teachers and their classmates, both for the benefit of their academic performance and social life, and to prevent the school from losing students.

    Furthermore, it may be useful for other institutions to avoid suffering the same issues.
